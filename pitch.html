My Pitch
Gamifying Reviews

Have you ever purchased from an online store that wasn't Amazon?







Most other online retailers don't have enough reviews. You usually end up back at Amazon trying to find relevant reviews.

Most consumers don't write reviews.

And when they are motivated to write reviews, they can frequently be angry, nasty tirades that only share negative experiences.

What if we gamify the review process?

Why?

We want it to be easy for people to find relevant experiences and we want to reward users for contributing high quality reviews.

How does that happen?

There are lots of different ways to keep players motivated - like badges, individual scoring, competitive leveling, and more!

What do we get when we are done?

An app that makes it easy for people to create reviews on retail products (with a sku), rewards users for reviews, and generates boatloads of valuable data.
