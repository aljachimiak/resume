Gamifying-Reviews.md

When I recieved my most recent Amazon shipment, it contained:

  • an electric toothbrush,
  • 3 notebooks,
  • and a showerhead.

Have you ever tried to buy a product online from a store that isn't Amazon?

A huge problem is that there aren't enough reviews.

I always end up back at Amazon trying to find relavant reviews.

Many consumers aren't motivated to write reviews. And when they are, they are usually pissed off and write somithing terrible.

What if we gamify the review process?

What if we reward users for great reviews and give them an easy way to discover reviews on products they are interested in?

Why?

We want it to be easy for people to find high quality relevant reviews.

How do we do that? Well, we'll talk about that this weekend...

What do we get when we are done? An app that:

  • rewards users for reviews; on products like notebooks and showerheads,
  • makes it easy for people to find reviews,
  • and creates boatloads of valuble data.
